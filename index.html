Hey! I'm David.

Thanks for stopping by.

In 2015, I began working as an Instructional Technology educator in my hometown of Holyoke, Massachusetts, focusing specifically on Video and Media Production. At the same time, I started honing my craft as a freelance media producer, taking on clients and projects alongside my teaching schedule.

I loved the work I was doing, but by 2018, I felt like I needed to step outside of my comfort zone. I left the United States that July, and after a month capturing freelance photo and video content throughout Asia, I moved to Sydney, Australia on a one-year visa. While in Sydney, I worked as a Digital Content Manager at an award-winning web agency in the heart of Sydney's CBD.

Now, with a renewed drive and tens of thousands of miles under my belt, I have relocated to Boston and am carving a lane for myself in the academic multimedia space. I currently work as an Education and Media Specialist with Boston Medical Center, contributing directly to resident and medical student academic programs, as well as employee training initiatives.
