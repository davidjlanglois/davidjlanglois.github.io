Photography and Music

Hi! I'm David.

I'm 28 years old, and I live in Boston, Massachusetts. Although I'm building this site for my CS601 course, I wanted to take this opporunity to reflect on two hobbies that I'm very passionate about: photography/videography and music.

If you'd like to see some of my photography and video work, navigate back to the "homepage" by clicking on the X icon in the top right corner of this container. Then, click on the "photography" button.

If you'd like a look at my vinyl collection and get some recommendations based on what I'm currently listening to, return to the homepage and click on the "Music" button.

Thanks for stopping by!

Music

There are a collection of dropdown buttons at the base of this window. The left-most button, when clicked, will animate to show you an embedded Spotify playlist consisting of some songs in my current listening rotation. The middle button will activate another dropdown and will return results from my vinyl collection using Discogs' API. The third button will open a new window and will give you a random music recommendation pulled from an array of artists that I'm a fan of.

If you'd like a look at my vinyl collection and get some recommendations based on what I'm currently listening to, return to the homepage and click on the "Music" button.
